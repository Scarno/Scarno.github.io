Update #2


December 15, 2018

Long time no see. This update came far later than I had hoped. A cold, my work ramping up, and bunch of new game releases ganged up on my free time so Project Iron worked slowed down a bit. That said, here we are! Since last time, I've added a proper sprint (distinct from the default run) as well as an attack animation.
